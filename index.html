My name is Nathaniel Liganor and I am a second year Psychology major at the University of California, Riverside. I am currently taking courses towards my degree such as Psychology, Political Science, and Media Cultural Studies. However, I plan on taking courses that fulfill the requirements needed for graduate school. I aspire to become a Physician Assitant and work under a Physician.

I was born in Quezon City, Philippines in the year 2000. I then moved to San Francisco, California with my parents and younger sister when I was only 5 years old where I grew up for most of my childhood. I attended School of the Epiphany for primary school. I then attended Junipero Serra High School where I graduated. I have worked as a math tutor and basketball coach over the past years which has taught me valuable life skills such as how to communicate with others, being patient and much more.

On campus, I am currently involved in UCR's Associated Student Programming Board (ASPB) where many of the school's events come from. Through ASPB I have learned a great deal about leadership and what it means to become a leader. I have developed phenomenal communication and teamwork skills. I am also a member of UCR's Katipunan, the school's Filipino Organization where I am able to connect with other people about my culture and background.
